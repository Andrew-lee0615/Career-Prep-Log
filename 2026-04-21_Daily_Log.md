#### **B. In-depth Data Interpretation**

**1. Scatter Plot Analysis: Visualizing Inverse Correlation**
- **Mechanism:** As the X-axis (Gas Returns) moves right, the Y-axis (Nasdaq Returns) tends to move downward, forming a **Negative Regression Line**.
- **Conclusion:** This visually proves that energy supply-side shocks act as a systemic cost pressure, negatively impacting the valuations of tech-heavy growth indices.


**2. The Nature of the 'Spread': Measuring Relative Strength**
- **Definition:** Calculated as `XLE Return - XLI Return`. It identifies which sector maintains relative strength during volatility, rather than just looking at absolute price movements.
- **Result (94bp):** During price spikes, Energy stocks either rose more or fell less compared to Industrials, which faced immediate margin compression.
- **Strategic Value:** The consistent positive spread provides a statistical foundation for a **"Long Energy / Short Industrials"** pairs trading strategy during geopolitical crises.


**3. Critical Review & Outlier Analysis**
